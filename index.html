ABOUT ME

I'm a UX/UI designer focused on connecting effortless user experience with beautiful interface design. After studying strategic communication at the Ohio State University, I began to work in fast paced environments to ensure client's success through effective communication. This experience taught me how to identify problems and create effective solutions.

Through the encouragement of family, friends, and my wife, I left a leading technology advisory firm to pursue a passion for designing responsive user experiences to help transform businesses.

Music is another passion of mine whether I'm playing it, creating it or just listening to it. My favorite band will forever be the Beatles. They're the best band of all time and no one will convince me otherwise.

Making a career change has been challenging but I'm so excited to move forward in my career as a UX/UI designer. Please explore the case studies for a more detailed look into my design process and feel free to contact me with any questions.
